Professional Organizations

Professional Organization: Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE)

Description: IEEE is a professional organization for electrical engineers, electronics engineers, and similar occupations. They are dedicated to the advancement of technology for the benefit of humanity. Computer Scientists in IEEE are usually more focused on the hardware aspect of Computer Science.

Professional Organization: The Association of Computing Machinery (ACM)

Description: ACM at UCLA is split up into 8 committees revolving around a specific topic of computing such as cybersecurity, machine learning, and competitive programming.

Professional Organization: Linux Users Group (Lug)

Description: The Linux Users Group at UCLA is a branch of a larger community that serves as a tool for students to learn all things Linux. The organization's primary targets are inexperienced Linux users who they will provide biweekly meetings for instruction..
